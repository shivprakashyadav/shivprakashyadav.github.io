Krishna Enterprise

Designing the Graphics for the World Engineering day Celebration held at Vapi in collaboration with Emagin Entertainment, Daman.

Background

Krishna Enterprise, Daman wanted to celebrate World Engineer's day , and they wanted us to do the design work for the event held at vapi.

The main theme for the event was appreciating the contribution and acknowledge of stalwart engineers that india has produced.

Dropdown Banner

Dropdowns were used in the main event area to showcase achievements and background of the engineers.

Stage Backdrop

A stage was built with a space given for projection. We designed an Backdrop with the theme of engineers day.

Broucher

We designed the broucher for the event , reflecting the ethos of Krishna Enterprise.

Leaflet

Leaflets used in the broucher to showcase different projects taken up by Krishna Enterprise.

Standee

We designed 2 standees for the event, one for directing the invited guests and other one reflecting different roles by engineers
